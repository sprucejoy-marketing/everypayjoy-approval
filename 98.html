12 Ways to Get Free Toys

As with any other way of getting freebies, some of these ways to get free toys may take a bit more legwork than others. Either way, using these options, you’ll get toys at no cost to you.

Once you get them, you can give ’em to your own kids or kids of family and friends. If you don’t have any kids around you, you can always giveaway toys especially during the holidays either directly or through programs like Toys for Tots.

Here are ways you can get totally free toys.

1. Become a toy tester

You may be able to get toys for your child to test out for free.

It sounds too good to be true, but testing out toys is totally possible.

Many toy companies hire kids to be toy testers. They send your child a toy to review, and in exchange for this review, you get to keep the toy.

There are a few ways to find toy testing work:

Follow toy manufacturers on social media

Follow toy companies big and small on social media. Often, companies post on their social media profiles that they’re looking for kids to test toys.

A few top brands you should follow are:

Step2: Facebook

Hasbro Gaming: Facebook

Mattel Imagination: Facebook

Spin Master: Facebook

Sign up for newsletters

Most toy manufacturers send out weekly/monthly newsletter where they give subscribers the latest news as well as exclusive discounts and promo codes. Some will also send you sample toys in the mail for free so you can test out and give feedback.

Here are a few newsletters you can sign up for free:

Fisher Price Family Club

Lego VIP

Get an online presence

A lot of people get product recommendations from blogs and social media.

And companies want to use that to market their products.

So, if a toy company sees that you have an active online presence and a large following, it’s more likely to send you toys to review.

You should set up a blog, YouTube channel and social media accounts if you don’t have them already.

The bigger the online reach you have, the more likely you are to be selected as a toy tester.

It’s a good idea to review the toys you already have and post your reviews to your blog. You can then use your blog as a portfolio to show companies that you can write good reviews and are able to thoroughly review products.

Visit the website of your favorite toy companies to get their contact information.

3. Get free toys in exchange for Amazon reviews

This is a little on the gray side. You see, sellers on Amazon can’t directly ask you for feedback in exchange for giving you free stuff. But there are ways to get free stuff in exchange for feedback.

You can sign up with review sites

Join review sites

There are sites that are specifically set up to bring sellers and buyers together where sellers will either giveaway 100% free products or give deep discounts in exchange for your reviews. We have a big list of Amazon review sites here.

Join Amazon Vine

Amazon Vine is Amazon’s own review program where you can get free stuff and exclusive deals and discounts for reviews. You can read “how to get free stuff on Amazon” for details and tips about the program.

Unfortunately, Amazon Vine is an invitation-only program. You can’t just sign up. In order to be invited, you’d have to have a lot of existing reviews (positive or negative) that other customers have found helpful.

So, if you haven’t yet, start leaving detailed and helpful reviews for every item you buy on Amazon. Maybe one day you’ll be invited. And even if you’re not invited to the Vine program, many sellers will actually contact you directly and offer you freebies in exchange for your detailed reviews. It’s a win-win!

4. Browse Freecycle

Freecycle is this great website where people can give away stuff for free and get free stuff too.

It’s an awesome website because anyone can use it to get their hands on the stuff they need at no cost.

Freecycle offers items in lots of categories. So, you may be able to find toys that people are giving away on there.

5. Look on Craigslist

The next place I’d recommend looking at is on Craigslist. Sure, a lot of people sell stuff on Craigslist. However, there are also lots of people who give away free toys and other products.

You just need to find the free section for your city.

Lots of cities have a section for free stuff, like:

Craigslist Bronx

Craigslist Manhattan

Craigslist San Francisco Bay Area

Craigslist Boston

Craigslist Chicago

Craigslist Los Angeles

I saw free stuffed animal toys on the LA free section, for example.

Now, you won’t always find people giving away toys at no cost. However, you sometimes will, so having a quick browse of your city’s free section on Craigslist is certainly a good idea.

6. Get free gift cards for toys from Swagbucks

I think that anyone who’s looking to get anything for free should be using Swagbucks.

If you haven’t heard about Swagbucks before it’s a website where you get rewarded for doing really easy tasks.

Yes, you read that right.

Swagbucks will pay you to do tasks that are easy and, in many cases, fun too!

I’d highly recommend using the site.

Here’s what it will pay you for:

Shopping online

Playing games

Watching videos

Browsing the internet

Completing offers

How great is that?

When you do these tasks, you’ll be rewarded with points that are called SB. You can then use your points to get money sent right to your PayPal account, cash on a Visa card, or gift cards.

There are lots of different gift card options available as well.

You can find gift cards for lots of retailers who sell toys, such as:

Amazon

Walmart

Target

So, basically, you get free money or gift cards you can use to get toys at no cost!

11. Get money back when you shop for toys through cashback websites

You can actually make money the next time you shop with cashback websites.

Cashback sites give you back a percentage of what you spend.

You could get anywhere from 1% to 70% or more cashback.

This means if a site offered you 20% cashback, you’d get $4 back if you spent $20.

So, you can get a big discount on toys. Or you can wait for your cashback to add up and use that money to get toys at no cost!

Here are some cashback websites to try:

Mr.Rebates

TopCashBack

Pennyful

12. Automatically save money on toys with browser extensions

Did you know that you could automatically save money when you’re shopping for toys online?

How great would that be?

Just download one of these awesome browser extensions:

Wikibuy

Extrabux

With these extensions, you can get coupons applied to your order automatically.

I know when shopping online that sometimes coupons don’t work. If you’re in a rush, you don’t really have the time to try loads of different coupons until you find one that works.

What’s great about these extensions is that they will find you coupons that actually work, saving you time and money!

Some also give you cashback!
