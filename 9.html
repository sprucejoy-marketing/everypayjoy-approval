Top Premium Pick-up Trucks of 2019

Pick-up trucks have become one of the most popular ways of transportation in recent years. It is reported that trucks are the safest way to transport passengers due to impact protection of the sides as well as other safety features including stability control.

Trucks provide room for passengers as well as a bed for storage. The following are some examples of the top premium pick-up trucks of 2019:

1. 2019 Ram 1500

The 2019 Ram 1500 can hold up to five people and has four doors. It comes with automatic load leveling, which caters to heavy towing jobs. It can tow up to 12,750 pounds on the road. The advanced rear camera can help you drive while towing safely. The high-strength steel helps keep a sturdy frame and also contributes to the towing capacity. This truck comes with an eTorque, a new hybrid alternator that is run by a battery and helps the engine towing capacity.

2. 2019 Chevrolet Silverado 1500

Chevrolet advertises this truck as the most dependable and most long lasting on the road at this time. It features built-in 4G LTE Wi-Fi. It also keeps a 22 mpg fuel economy on the highway. 3. 2019 Ford F-150

The Ford F-150 features a high-tech back-up camera with hitch technologies as well for a safe towing trip. 4. 2019 GMC Sierra 1500

The 2019 GMC Sierra 1500 has back-up technology as well, only with this truck, you can keep your head up. The back-up camera is actually on the rear-view mirror, which helps you simultaneously look for yourself and use the camera. This truck also features the most cargo room out of any other competing truck. It comes with mud-terrain Goodyear tires as well. This truck is available in different shades of black and white.
