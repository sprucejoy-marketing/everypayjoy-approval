Best-Selling SUV Crossovers

With utility and plenty of cargo space, fuel efficient, affordable, the benefits of an SUV Crossover are practically endless. When a family is looking for passenger room, as well as having space for the needs of extra-curricular activities, groceries, suitcases, backpacks, even the dog, they are not looking for a full size truck, they are looking for a family-oriented vehicle that excels in amenities and benefits. 

That is exactly what SUV crossovers offer families today and why they have become quite popular. Here are the best-selling crossover SUVs:

1. BMW X2. Cadillac XT4

Plenty of headroom and legroom, front seat comfort is plentiful. Extraordinarily cost efficient when it comes to when it comes to filling up, with 23 to 24 miles to the gallon in the city, and 30 to 32 on the highway. Seating 5 comfortably along with your extras, also providing a slightly smaller turning radius allowing you to easily maneuver in and out of every space. Stylish, sporty, yet fully functional. Your passengers will be able to stretch out comfortably, and you won’t have to stop as frequently for fuel as the BMW X2. Cadillac XT4 gives you 22 to 24 miles to the gallon in the city and 29 to 30 on the highway. Seating 5, again along with the extras, this vehicle also adds a little extra horsepower to your driving experience. Chic to the eye, this crossover provides style and versatility.

2. Volkswagen Tiguan 

With 5 to 7 seats, first and second row, plus the cargo space, the Volkswagen Tiguan is a great choice. With fuel efficiency of 24 to 25 miles per gallon, and the ability to create a third row, your drive will also be a smooth one. Uniquely crisp handling, responsive steering, not only is this vehicle sleek on the outside, but the premium interior is a great plus.

3. Volvo XC40

Upscale look, the Volvo XC40 comes equipped with generous storage and smart technology. Seating capacity of 5, very comfortably, this SUV Crossover has a unique folding floor making the load compartment even more versatile. Fuel efficient, receiving 23 to 31 to 26 miles to the gallon city and highway driving combined, technology has given way to both performance and versatility.

4. Audi Q8

This midsize luxury SUV comes fully equipped to seat 5, receives 19 miles to the gallon combined, and not only has that luxury look, but the leather upholstery is a great plus. With impressionable cargo space, Audi Q8 comes complete with safety and driver assistance technology and provides exactly what a family would be looking for in a practical, yet sporty family car.

5. Mazda CX-5

With a seating capacity of 5, gas mileage of 22 to 25 in the city, 27 to 31 highway, plus the fact it’s one of the most athletic and safe in its class, the Mazda CX-5 is certainly a great choice. Handling with excellence in combination with its energetic engine, this is a high quality vehicle. Ample cargo space for those extras, and comfortable seating, its cabin outstanding, the benefits and features are exceptional.

These are just to name a few of the SUV Crossovers available on the market. Safe, efficient, quality, versatility, the popularity is not a wonder when looking for a family vehicle that not only provides what you need, but all have a sporty, sleek style.
